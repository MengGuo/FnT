\chapter{Dynamic Uncertainty and Constraints}
\label{chap:uncertainty}

Chapters~\ref{chap:bottom-up}, \ref{chap:top-down}, and
\ref{chap:communication} developed coordination frameworks for temporal-task
planning under local interactions, global specifications, and limited
communication. This chapter considers the additional difficulty caused by
uncertain environments and time-varying constraints: propositions may be
stochastic, targets may move, feasible motions may change, and safety may
require recoverability rather than state avoidance alone.
The chapter is organized around three problem classes.
Section~\ref{ch06:sec:ch06_safe_plan} studies probabilistic temporal tasks with
safe-return requirements. Section~\ref{ch06:sec:ch06_uncertain} considers
reactive coordination under dynamic temporal tasks with prediction, online
reassignment, and moving targets. Section~\ref{ch06:sec:ch06_stl} addresses
continuous multi-robot motion planning under STL specifications using
collaborative optimal transport. The resulting methods are illustrated in
Section~\ref{ch06:sec:case}.
